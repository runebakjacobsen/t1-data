## 3. Training Schedule & Framework

### Weekly Volume Target

**Baseline (current):** ~3–4 hours/week
**Target:** progressing toward 8+ hours/week
**Peak phases:** scale up only when readiness supports it (RI ≥ 0.8, HRV within 10%)

> Progression note: ramp volume gradually (roughly ≤ 5–10%/week) to keep the build sustainable while moving from 3–4h toward 8h.

### Normal Weekly Schedule (representative — adapt freely)

Structure: 1–2 hard sessions, the rest easy. No off-limits days. Sample shape at current volume:

| Day       | Primary Session         | Duration | Secondary       |
| --------- | ----------------------- | -------- | --------------- |
| Monday    | Rest or easy spin       | —        | Strength        |
| Tuesday   | Hard session (threshold/VO₂) | 60–75 min | —          |
| Wednesday | Endurance (Z2)          | 45–60 min| —               |
| Thursday  | Rest or easy spin       | —        | Strength        |
| Friday    | Hard session (intervals)| 60–75 min| —               |
| Saturday  | Long endurance ride     | 90+ min  | —               |
| Sunday    | Easy / recovery         | optional | —               |

> This is a starting template, not a fixed plan — your coach will adjust day-to-day based on readiness and your schedule.

### Recovery Protocol

**Recovery Triggers (Auto-Deload):**

- HRV ↓ > 20% → reduce intensity / swap to easy
- RHR ↑ ≥ 5 bpm → ease back, monitor
- Feel ≥ 4 → reduce load
- Two+ triggers → deload day

**Feel Scale:**

| Score | Meaning                                      |
| ----- | -------------------------------------------- |
| 1     | Excellent (fully recovered)                  |
| 2     | Good (normal fatigue)                        |
| 3     | Moderate (manageable tiredness)              |
| 4     | Fatigued (reduced readiness, deload trigger) |
| 5     | Exhausted (complete rest required)           |
